Brief History

Welcome to our school

TAI SOLARIN SCHOOL Lagos was established in 1996, first in Delta state then 1997 in Festac Town Lagos. The school is owned by the Late Chief Patrick O. Iwediuno in memory of the late educationist Tai Solarin, to immortalize his name and provide quality education, that's affordable.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them as lifelong learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school: Respect, Creativity, Excellence and Entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

Our goal as a school is to equip our students with the skills and mindset to thrive, succeed and then take on the world
